Product Designer

My work lives where product design, data visualization, and storytelling meet. I build dashboards, interactive tools, and visual systems that turn dense data into something people can actually navigate, trust, and act on.

I started out designing B2C experiences and moved into enterprise SaaS and data-heavy products. That range taught me a lot: how to balance what users need, what the business is after, what's technically possible, and how genuinely complicated real systems get.

Design keeps changing, and I like keeping up with it — poking at emerging tech, AI-driven experiences, and new ways to make complicated information feel approachable. Whatever I'm working on, the goal's the same: bring clarity to complexity, and help people get from insight to action.
