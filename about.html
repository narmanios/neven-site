Product & Data Visualization Designer

I'm Neven Armanios, a Senior Product Designer focused on complex data, AI-assisted workflows, and research tools. I design clear, usable experiences that help people understand information, uncover insights, and make better decisions.

My work sits at the intersection of product design, data visualization, and storytelling. I create dashboards, interactive tools, and visual systems that turn complex data into experiences that are easier to navigate, trust, and act on.

My career has evolved from designing B2C experiences to building products for enterprise SaaS teams and data-heavy workflows. This range has shaped the way I approach design: balancing user needs, business goals, technical constraints, and the complexity of real-world systems.

As design continues to evolve, I continue to adapt by exploring emerging technologies, AI-driven experiences, and new ways of making complex information more accessible. Across my work, my goal is to design products that bring clarity to complexity and help people move from insight to action.
